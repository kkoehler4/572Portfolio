Next Stop: Madison


Created for my Introduction to Cartography class, this is a classed chloropleth map meant to serve as an educational tool for training the public on how to interpret statistics and the maps that represent them. Curious about how access to transportation differs throughout the city, I decided to make my map on average daily bus boardings in Madison. I used blue, orange and red to mimic the characteric colors of Madison Metro Transit buses and signs.
