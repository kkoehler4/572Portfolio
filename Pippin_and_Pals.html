Pippin and Pals


I created Pippin and Pals as the final project for my Graphic Design in Cartography class. Growing up an on apple orchard, I was able to learn a lot about the way animals, trees, and people interact throughout the seasons just by exploring and playing in the orchard. For my final project, I wanted to provide an educational resource that allows other kids to explore and learn from my family's orchard, Windfall Orchard, in a fun, interactive way through cute characters and a (hopefully) funny storyline. ESRI Story Maps was a great way to bring my characters and story to life; using the "sidecar" feature, I was able to place images in succession to give the impression of movement and change. I hope that my project allows kids to learn a bit about an apple orchard as they make some (friendly) monster friends. Click on the picture to view the story!
