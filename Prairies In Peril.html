Prairies in Peril


Created for my Graphic Design in Cartography class, this project was inspired by my restoration ecology internship with the Madison Audubon. With this longform infographic, I hoped to convey the beauty and importance of native prairies in Wisconsin. ESRI StoryMaps allowed me to develop a narrative surrounding these prairies using both text and images, beginning with history of prairies and their significance to native peoples of Wisconsin, followed by the arrival of European settlers and the subsequent prairie loss, and concluding with various ways to give back to the prairies. I hope this project both informs and inspires readers to contribute to the protection and restoration of native landscapes. Click on the image to read the whole story!
