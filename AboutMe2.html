About Me


Hello! I am a junior at UW-Madison studying Spanish and People-Environment Geography. I am interested in bilingual and environmental education. In the past, I have worked for Creando Little Language Explorers, a Spanish immersion preschool and afterschool program, the Madison Audubon Society, the Vermont Youth Conservation Corps, and the Middlebury Area Land Trust. When I'm not at school or work, you can usually find me climbing trees, cross-country skiing, painting rocks like fruit, or trying to crochet a sloth.
