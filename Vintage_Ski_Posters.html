Vintage Ski Poster


Created for my Graphic Design in Cartography class, this project intended to replicate the visual style of vintage ski posters. First, I identified several posters for inspiration and deconstructed their style into constituent forms, colors, types, and textures. Then, I applied these components to a map using Mapbox Studio, paying close attention to how individual features like roads and waterways could imitate the style of the posters I used as inspiration. Finally, I created a set of icons that corresponded to points of interest important to skiers and once again reinforced the basemap style.
