Nordic Ski Team Badger


This year, I am the Apparel Director for the UW-Madison Hoofers Nordic Ski Team. My tasks involve designing, ordering, and distributing apparel. As a club team, we aren't allowed to use Bucky Badger, so I decided to design us a custom badger. His face is meant to be friendly and cute (unlike the offical Bucky), as we are a welcoming team open to all different levels of skiiers. The badger's hat is based off of the hat from the Birkibeiner, the largest cross-country ski race in North America located in Hayward, Wisconsin.
